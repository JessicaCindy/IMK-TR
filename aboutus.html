ABOUT US

Lavela started from a simple dream of a housewife who loved to bake cakes for her family and neighbors. In early 2021, in the midst of a challenging pandemic, a desire arose to turn a hobby into a more serious business opportunity. With a family recipe that has been passed down from generation to generation, the name "Lavela" is taken from a combination of words that mean warmth, love, and deliciousness—three things that we always want to present in every bite.

More Than Bread — It’s an Experience
We believe that every bite should leave a lasting impression. That’s why we use only premium ingredients and craft each loaf with heart and care. From the careful selection of materials to the final bake, our bread is made with love to deliver a warm, soft, and unforgettable taste.
